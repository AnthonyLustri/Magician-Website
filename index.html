About the Magician

John Michaels has been performing magic for the past 45 years, he takes some classics of magic combined with comedy, music and showmanship to present an exciting and colorful show that is enjoyed by all ages. John Michaels has been entertaining groups for corporate events, private parties, variety shows, theme parks and schools.

School Assembly Shows

From 1999 to 2008 John Michaels traveled throughout Ohio, Indiana, Michigan, and Pennsylvania, working with the National Child Safety Council and the National Fire Safety Council performing as "Johnny Magic," combining magic with important safety messages and performing over 2,500 school show assemblies.

Prior to that, John Michaels performed his own school assembly programs.
