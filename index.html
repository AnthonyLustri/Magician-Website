About the Magician

John Michaels has been performing magic for the past 45 years. He takes some classics of magic combined with comedy, music, and showmanship to present an exciting and colorful show that is enjoyed by all ages.

Michael's Show is great entertainment for!

  • • Corporate Events
  • • Theme Parks
  • • Fundraisers
  • • Private Shows

Admired by his peers in magic, John Michaels has been asked to perform his acts at such magic conventions as the Texas Association of Magicians, Columbus Ohio's famed Magi-Fest, New York's and Canada's Associations of Magicians, and Appleton, Wisconsin’s Houdini Convention.

So if you are in the need of entertainment for a large venue or just a small audience, The John Michaels Show can custom tailor a show for your next event.

National Child Safety Council

From 1999 to 2008 John Michaels traveled throughout Ohio, Indiana, Michigan, and Pennsylvania, working with the National Child Safety Council and the National Fire Safety Council performing as "Johnny Magic," combining magic with an important safety message and performing over 2,500 school show assemblies.

Prior to that, John Michaels performed his own school assembly programs.
