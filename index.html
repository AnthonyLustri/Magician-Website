About the Magician

John Michaels takes some classes of magic combined with comedy, music, and showmanship to present an exciting and colorful show that is enjoyed by all ages.

Michael’s Show is great entertainment for:

  • • Corporate Events
  • • Fundraisers
  • • Private Shows

Admired by his peers in magic, John Michaels has been asked to perform his act at such magic conventions as the Texas Association of Magicians, Columbus, Ohio’s famed Magi-Fest, New York and Canada’s Associations of Magicians, and Appleton, Wisconsin’s Houdini Convention.

So if you are in need of entertainment for a large venue or just a small audience, The John Michaels Show can custom tailor a performance for your next event.
